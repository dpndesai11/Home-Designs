# Home Designs — 3D Printable Home Accessories

A personal collection of custom-designed 3D printable parts made to solve small practical problems around the house. Each design was modelled from scratch and printed at home.

---

## Designs

### Blind Hook (`Blindhook.stl`)
A replacement or additional hook for window blinds. Designed to fit standard blind cords and wall-mount cleanly without requiring screws or adhesive.

### Pen Holder (`Pen Holder.stl`)
A compact desk pen holder. Sized to hold standard pens and pencils upright, keeping a desk surface clear.

### Toothbrush Holder (`Toothbrush holder.stl`)
A wall-mountable toothbrush holder for the bathroom. Holds a standard electric or manual toothbrush.

---

## How to Print

All files are provided as `.stl` — import directly into your slicer of choice (Cura, PrusaSlicer, Bambu Studio, etc.).

Recommended settings:
- **Material:** PLA or PETG
- **Layer height:** 0.2mm
- **Infill:** 15–20% for decorative parts, 30%+ for load-bearing parts (blind hook)
- **Supports:** Check in slicer — may be needed depending on orientation
